Across more than two decades and five institutions, Dimitrios has taught the full systems curriculum — computer organization and architecture, operating systems, parallel and multicore programming, and warehouse-scale and AI systems — from first-year undergraduate courses to advanced graduate seminars. His Virginia Tech courses consistently earn strong student evaluations (around 5.3 / 6).
